Literary Events — Express Your Words

Intercollegiate Literary Fest — 26th to 28th November 2025

Poetry / Shayari

Rules — Poetry / Shayari

  • Individual event.
  • Original compositions preferred.
  • Duration: 2–3 minutes per participant.
  • Offensive or plagiarized content not allowed.
  • Judgment based on creativity and expression.

JAM (Just A Minute)

Rules — JAM

  • Individual event.
  • Topics given on the spot.
  • Speak for exactly one minute.
  • Hesitation, deviation, or repetition disqualifies.
  • Judged on fluency and confidence.

Elocution

Rules — Elocution

  • Individual event.
  • Topics provided beforehand.
  • Time limit: 3 minutes.
  • Use of notes not allowed.
  • Judgment on content, delivery, and clarity.
