Performing Arts — Live Stage Events on 28th November 2025

Solo Dance

Rules — Solo Dance

  • Duration: 3 to 5 minutes.
  • Props allowed but must be managed by participant.
  • Music to be submitted in advance (MP3 format).
  • Vulgarity in lyrics or steps will lead to disqualification.
  • Judgment based on choreography, expression, and energy.

Singing

Rules — Singing

  • Solo and duet categories allowed.
  • Time limit: 3 minutes.
  • Participants must bring their karaoke/instrumental track.
  • Language open but maintain decency.
  • Judgment based on vocal quality and expression.

Rap Royale (Rap)

Rules — Rap Royale

  • Original compositions preferred.
  • Time limit: 2 minutes.
  • No explicit or offensive lyrics.
  • Beatboxing or backing track allowed.
  • Judgment based on flow, clarity, and creativity.

BEAT RUSH (BEATBOXING)

Rules — BEAT RUSH (BEATBOXING)e

  • Maximum number of participantsin group category is 4 only.
  • Participantsshould avoid yelling,screaming during a competitor's routine. Be quiet during your
  • opponent's round.
  • Be mindful of the audience, and limit the use of profanity.
  • Performances are limited by time 2 minutes.
  • Beatboxers are judged on their technique,style, and musical creativity

Stand Up Comedy

Rules — Stand Up Comedy

  • Time limit: 5 minutes.
  • Solo performance only.
  • No vulgar or religious jokes.
  • Original content encouraged.
  • Judgment based on timing, originality, and delivery.
